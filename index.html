FAQs

Why is branding important?

Branding is the process of creating a unique identity for your business, including its logo, messaging, and overall image. A strong brand helps build recognition, trust, and loyalty, setting you apart from competitors and creating lasting connections with your audience.

What is included in thinkB’s branding package?

Our branding packages typically include logo design, color palettes, typography selection, brand guidelines, and collateral designs (such as business cards, letterheads, etc.). We can customise it as per your needs.

How does thinkB ensure that branding fits the business vision?

We begin with in-depth discussions to understand your business, goals, values, and target audience. We then create a tailored brand strategy that reflects your vision and ensures consistency across all brand touchpoints.

How much does branding cost?

The cost of branding varies depending on the scope of the project. We offer custom packages tailored to your specific needs and budget. We’d be happy to discuss your project in detail, fill the form shared above and we’ll get back to you.
